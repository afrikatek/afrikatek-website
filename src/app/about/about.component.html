Our Story at Afrikatek

Who We Are

Founded in 2018, Afrikatek Solutions is a leading digital technology company based in Nairobi, Kenya, with a growing presence across Africa. We started with a simple mission: to provide innovative, resilient, and scalable digital solutions that empower African businesses to compete globally.

Our team consists of passionate technologists, creative designers, and strategic thinkers who are committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake. We believe in the transformative power of technology and its ability to solve complex business challenges.
